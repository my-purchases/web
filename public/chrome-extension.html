Chrome Extension

My Purchases Collector

Automatically collect and export your AliExpress orders. No manual copy-pasting — just browse your orders and the extension captures everything.

Install from Chrome Web Store View Source on GitHub

How It Works

1

Browse your orders

Go to your AliExpress order history page. The extension automatically detects and captures order data from the page.

2

Collect all orders

Click "Collect All" in the extension popup to automatically scroll through and load all your orders.

3

Export your data

Export to CSV, JSON, HTML, or copy directly to clipboard for Google Sheets. Import into My Resources web app.

Features

Automatic API interception — no scraping, no manual work
One-click "Collect All" to load your entire order history
Export to CSV, JSON, HTML, or clipboard
Compatible with My Resources web app import
All data stays in your browser — zero tracking
Open source under MIT license

Requirements

  • Google Chrome (or Chromium-based browser)
  • AliExpress account with order history

Privacy

The extension stores all data locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. No analytics or tracking. Read the full privacy policy.
